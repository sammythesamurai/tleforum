CEO & Co-Founder of TLE, Samuel Yates is a 22 year old tech entreprenuer who grabbed his love of music and artisty and planted a tech foundation beneath it. Samuel interned at Expressive Artists for Albuquerque, New Mexico's Michael Davenport in 2009. While Samuel has been raised and lived in a variety of states and cities all over the United States, he calls home to Albuquerque, NM and Chicago,IL. Samuel is a Whitney Young High School Alumni and a current student at DePaul University & The Starter League. Samuel's inspirations come from his father, William Yates MD, Michael Jackson, The Weeknd, Steve Jobs, and Walt Disney.
